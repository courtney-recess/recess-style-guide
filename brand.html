Brand Values

The six pillars that guide our decisions, our culture, and our product.

🎭

We Bring People Together Through Experiences

Live events create real human connection. Everything we build is in service of making those moments happen more easily and more often.

🌱

We Remember Our Roots

We started as event organizers and sponsors ourselves. That first-hand experience keeps us grounded and honest about what the industry actually needs.

🔧

We Are Scrappy

We move fast, think creatively, and ship with urgency. Resourcefulness beats perfection every time.

⚖️

We Are Balanced

Hard work matters, but so does rest. We protect sustainable pace because burned-out teams don't build great products.

🎉

We Have Fun

Our industry is literally about fun. If the work itself doesn't spark joy, something's off. We celebrate wins, crack jokes, and never take ourselves too seriously.

📚

We Are Lifelong Learners

Curiosity is a core competency. We ask questions, share knowledge freely, and treat every failure as a lesson.

Logo Guidelines

How to use the Recess wordmark consistently across all touchpoints.

Aqua on white (preferred)

White on aqua

White on dark

✓

Preferred placement
Use on white or light backgrounds, or on the Aqua brand color.

✓

Dark backgrounds
Use the white version of the logo on navy or dark photography.

✓

Clear space
Maintain 150% of the logo width as clear space on all sides.

✗

Never alter the logo
Do not rotate, stretch, skew, recolor, add effects, or modify the wordmark in any way.
